Parking

As of November 26, 2015, the City of Edmonton has implemented a 'No Stopping' zone 24 hours a day along Stony Plain Road between 126th Street and 124th Street, and removed the meters.

There is a lot across the street available at 125th and Stony Plain Road for $3/hour weekdays and $1/hour Saturdays, free on evenings and Sundays.
