About the tea girl

The tea girl is an independently owned teahouse. Located in Edmonton off the corner of 124 Street and Stony Plain Road.

Hours of Operation:

Open 7 days a week:

10 am – 10 pm Monday to Sunday

Sit, enjoy tea or grab a quick bite to eat.

We have a fantastic selection of tea, food items and tea-themed gifts. The tea girl has over 100ntea and tea-blend choices.

If you need help, don’t be afraid to ask one of our lovely team members. Our staff complete extensive training and are very knowledgeable. They can help you choose a tea to suite your palate.

We have a kitchen on site where we bake almost all of our own desserts. We have a daily soup selection and chili as well as a few sandwich selections for a light lunch menu. We are often trying out new food options for our menu selections.

Allergies or other sensitivities:

Although we are not a gluten-free facility, we offer a wide selection store baked gluten-free and wheat-free items. We also have some dairy free and egg free items, and vegan selections.

Some teas and food items contain nuts.

Recent Expansion!

Recently The Tea Girl expanded by taking over the space next door. We added in a kitchen to bake our food on site and now have double the window space, which brightens our space with full natural light. We added seating but still kept our cozy decor and atmosphere.

Meetings, Groups and Special Events

We welcome everyone at the tea house, and we feel it is a great place to meet with a group. If you plan to come with a group of more than 10, calling ahead can help us accommodate you. We do have some larger tables that are well suited for fitting more people around them. We do not offer space for private functions, or host open mics or private music groups. Our atmosphere is geared towards connection and conversation, and we would like to allow our customers that opportunity any time they visit. We do however participate in many 124th Street sponsored events like Make Music Edmonton, The Red Shoe Crawl, and All is Bright if you want to catch a special event at the store.
